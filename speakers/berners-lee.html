Tim Berners-Lee

August 4th, 3-5 PM

Sir Tim Berners-Lee is the creator of the world wide web. He still works today as the director of the W3C and Web Science Research Initiative. He created the first website, which was launched on August 6th, 1991, and the internet has only grown since. He thinks that privacy is important and has ideas on what needs to be fixed.
