About

Priv DC is a conference taking place in Washington, DC, on August 4th and 5th in 2018. It is about privacy and surveillance today and in the future, and will feature several well-known speakers, including Laura Poitras, Edward Snowden, and Bruce Schneier. Along with the talks, there will also be events on how to protect your online life from unnecessary surveillance, and how to keep your life as private as you wish. The schedule and full list of speakers, along with detailed venue information and registration are available today.
